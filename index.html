Our Mission

We're on a mission to help people reclaim their focus in a world full of distractions. By combining cutting-edge brainwave sensing with real-time AI feedback, we're building a wearable that empowers users to understand their mental state, boost productivity, and form deeper, distraction-free work habits. Whether you're studying for exams, driving long hours, or deep in creative flow — we're here to keep your mind on track.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental focus is understood, measured, and enhanced — just like physical health. Our goal is to make neurotechnology accessible and practical, helping individuals and industries stay alert, engaged, and in control of their cognitive wellbeing. From students to truck drivers, we want to redefine how the world thinks about attention and productivity.

The Problem We're Solving

Work & Focus (B2C)

In an age of constant digital distraction, attention spans are shrinking. Social media, notifications, and overstimulation have made it difficult for people to stay focused on deep work. This leads to lost productivity, poor study outcomes, and burnout — with no clear way to measure or improve mental focus in real time.

Trucking & Safety (B2B)

Long-haul driving is a high-risk job where fatigue and loss of attention are leading causes of accidents. Drivers often push through exhaustion without realizing their cognitive state is deteriorating. There's a lack of real-time, non-intrusive tools to detect when a driver is losing focus — putting lives and cargo at risk.
